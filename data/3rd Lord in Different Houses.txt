3rd Lord in Different Houses
1st House
If the 3rd Lord is situated in the Ascendant, the native will have self-made wealth, be disposed to serve, valorous, and intelligent, although devoid of learning.
This is also a combination for actors, musicians, and dancers.
2nd House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 2nd House, the native will be corpulent, devoid of valor, less disposed to take initiative, unhappy, and will have eyes on others' wives and wealth.
3rd House
If the 3rd Lord occupies the 3rd House, the native will be happy through brothers, wealthy, have sons, and be cheerful and extremely happy.
This placement is excellent for hobbies and sports. If benefic planets are present, it promotes singing and voice-related careers.
Example: Lata Mangeshkar had the 3rd Lord Moon in the 3rd House.
4th House
If the 3rd Lord is situated in the 4th House, the native will be happy, wealthy, and intelligent.
If afflicted, they may lose land and live on others' property.
5th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 5th House, the native will have good relations with brothers, hold government positions, and be blessed with sons.
If aspected by a malefic, the native’s wife may be cruel.
A weak 3rd Lord may cause delayed or denied childbirth.
6th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 6th House, the native will be good at sports and athletics.
They may create enemies due to speech and have enmity with maternal uncles while loving maternal aunts.
7th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 7th House, the native’s brother may live abroad and succeed.
The native may have a government service career and gain happiness later in life despite childhood struggles.
A weak 3rd Lord may cause illicit relationships and government punishments.
8th House
A strong 3rd Lord in the 8th House indicates excellence in research work.
A weak 3rd Lord can cause health issues, criminal behavior, and a troubled married life.
9th House
A strong 3rd Lord in the 9th House signifies good relations with brothers, long journeys, and foreign travel.
The native may gain fortune through the spouse and be blessed with sons.
A weak Lord can cause paternal unhappiness.
10th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 10th House, the native will gain happiness, self-made wealth, and may engage in performing arts like singing, acting, or dancing.
11th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 11th House, the native will succeed in trade, be intelligent, and adventurous.
A weak 3rd Lord indicates serving others.
12th House
If the 3rd Lord is in the 12th House, the native may stay abroad, spend wealth on evil deeds, and have a cruel father.
The native may gain fortune through the spouse.
