Mars
Mars is a fire element and a warrior.
It provides courage, valor, and power.
Mars controls blood and marrow.
It symbolizes desire and action, being the only planetary symbol representing action.
Fearless, powerful, strong, and courageous, Mars has physical stamina and the capacity to achieve goals.
How a person appears outwardly, how aggressive they seem, or how they pursue their goals depends on Mars's placement in the birth chart.
Element: Copper
Associated professions include technical education, engineering, military, police force, fire-related work, land and property, electronics, and electrical fields.
Astrological Attributes of Mars
Causes blood-related diseases and fire-related issues like acidity, stomach problems, and surgeries.
Mars rules two rashis: Mesh (Aries) and Vrishchika (Scorpio).
Aspects: 4th, 7th, and 8th houses.
Direction: South
Karka (significator): Younger brother
Metal: Copper, Gem: Coral, Color: Red
Stay in a Rashi: 45 days
