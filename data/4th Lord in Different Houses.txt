4th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
The native is blessed with learning, virtues, ornaments, lands, conveyances, and maternal happiness.
2nd House
The native will enjoy wealth, family life, and honor but may be cunning and deceptive.
3rd House
If the 4th Lord is in the 3rd House, the native will be valorous, possess self-earned wealth, and be charitable.
They may face worries due to the house being the 12th from its own position.
4th House
If the 4th Lord occupies the 4th House, the native will rise to ministerial rank, possess wealth, and be skillful, virtuous, honorable, learned, and happy.
They will gain maternal happiness, property, and vehicles.
Success in agriculture is likely.
5th House
The native will have a sharp mind, many houses, and wealth.
They will be a devotee of Lord Vishnu, virtuous, honorable, and possess self-earned wealth.
6th House
Conflicts with the mother and legal issues related to property may arise.
If the 4th Lord is afflicted, the native will be devoid of maternal happiness, have a wrathful temperament, and indulge in questionable activities.
7th House
If the 4th Lord is in the 7th House, the native will be highly learned and may leave paternal property.
They may gain property through the spouse and succeed in business.
8th House
If the 4th Lord is in the 8th House, the native may be devoid of comforts and maternal happiness.
This placement may bring parental property or hidden treasures if well-placed.
If afflicted, it may cause property disputes and maternal accidents.
9th House
If the 4th Lord is in the 9th House, the native will be loved by all, be religious, virtuous, honorable, and enjoy all kinds of happiness.
10th House
The native will enjoy royal honors, possess great skills, be pleased, experience pleasure, and control the five senses.
11th House
The native may fear secret diseases but be liberal, virtuous, charitable, and helpful to others.
12th House
If the 4th Lord is in the 12th House, the native will lack domestic comforts, indulge in vices, and be foolish and indolent.
This is favorable for foreign residence and owning a home abroad.
