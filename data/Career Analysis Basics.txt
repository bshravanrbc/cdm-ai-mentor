Career Analysis Basics

Use the following rules to interpret a person's career based on planetary placements:

- 10th house + Dashamesh → 10th house shows karma direction; not necessarily profession alone.
- If 10th house has benefic planets → good career.
- 6th house → Job/Service; 7th house → Business/Self-employment.
- Dasha of 6th house or planets in it → Career likely in job/service.
- 5th house → Education/professional learning; 4th → general education.
- 5th lord not connected to 10th → career different from field of study.
- Check which house (6th, 7th, Lagna) has more weight from planets:
  - If 6th dominates → job; if 7th → business.

**Doctor:**
- 12th house → Hospital link.
- 6th house → Disease/healing.
- 5th & 9th house → Medical education.

**Engineer:**
- Malefic planets in 4th/5th → engineering tendencies.
- 5th house linked with 10th → person becomes engineer.
- Ketu → AI, deep tech.
- Venus → Aeronautics.
- Mercury → Communication Engineering.

**Actor:**
- 3rd house → Performance, communication.
- Venus + Moon → Expression, emotions.
- 2nd + 3rd + 5th + 10th house connection → Strong for acting.
- Venus' impact on Lagna/Lagna Lord or 10L/10H → Key indicator.

**Lawyer / Admin / Politics:**
- Strong Sun, Saturn, 10th house connection.
- Jupiter + 9th house influence → Public guidance.
- Malavya Yoga → Politics, Luxury industries.
- Gaja Kesari Yoga → Teaching, Influence, Public speaking.

**General:**
- Rashi Aries → Defense, Engineering
- Taurus → Business, Finance
- Gemini → Journalism, Writing
- Cancer → Nursing, Counseling
- Leo → Politics, Government roles
- Virgo → Analysis, Teaching
- Libra → Arts, Fashion
- Scorpio → Surgery, Intelligence
- Sagittarius → Law, Religion
- Capricorn → Management, Bureaucracy
- Aquarius → Innovation, Social Service
- Pisces → Spiritual roles, Healing

Evaluate the Lagna, 10th house, Dashamesh, karakas, and yogas.
