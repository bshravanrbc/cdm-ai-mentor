5th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
If the 5th Lord occupies the Ascendant, the native will be scholarly, blessed with children, and enjoy happiness.
He will be a master of many servants, a judge or minister, and religious.
If weak, the native will be miserly, crooked, and prone to stealing others' wealth.
2nd House
If the 5th Lord occupies the 2nd House, the native will have many sons and wealth.
He will be a supporter of his family, honorable, attached to his wife, and famous in the world.
If weak, family life will be disturbed, poverty may result, and problems related to the lungs can arise.
3rd House
If the 5th Lord is situated in the 3rd House, the native will be dear to his brothers, a tale-bearer, miserly, and always interested in his own work.
If weak, the native may face problems with children and be deceitful.
4th House
If the 5th Lord occupies the 4th House, the native will be happy, have maternal happiness, wealth, and intelligence, and be a king, minister, or preceptor.
This is a good combination of Kendra and Trine (Trikona).
As the 4th House is the 12th from the 5th, it can cause loss of children.
5th House
If the 5th Lord is placed in the 5th House and conjunct with a benefic, the native will be blessed with sons, virtuous, and dear to friends.
This is a good combination for profession, education, and close government connections, especially for mathematicians and religious leaders.
If conjunct with a malefic, it can harm children’s longevity, cause fickle-mindedness, anger, and harsh dealings.
6th House
If the 5th Lord is in the 6th House, the native’s sons will be like enemies or may die.
The native may acquire an adopted or purchased son.
This placement can cause breaks in education, delays, or denial of children.
7th House
If the 5th Lord is situated in the 7th House, the native will be tolerant of all religions, religious, and helpful to others.
It indicates love marriage and the possibility of a son settling abroad.
If weak, there will be a loss of children and love.
8th House
If the 5th Lord is in the 8th House, the native will have less progenic happiness and suffer from respiratory diseases, lung problems, and bad finances.
Breaks in education and love affairs are likely.
This is favorable for spiritual growth, mantra sadhana, and kundalini awakening.
9th House
If the 5th Lord is placed in the 9th House, the native will be like a prince or an illustrious author.
It is an excellent combination for writing and spiritual practices.
If weak, it can cause misfortune and bad luck.
10th House
If the 5th Lord occupies the 10th House, the native will be blessed with Raj Yoga, governmental favor, pleasures, and fame.
This combination ensures work in the same field as the native’s education, ideal for self-employment.
If weak, education and profession suffer.
11th House
If the 5th Lord is in the 11th House, the native will be learned, popular, an illustrious author, skillful, and blessed with many sons and wealth.
If weak, the native will earn money with difficulty and have shallow knowledge.
12th House
If the 5th Lord is in the 12th House, the native will lack happiness from sons, and children may live far away.
The 5th House represents past life merits, while the 12th signifies Moksha, making this placement ideal for spiritual growth.
The native will visit various religious places.
