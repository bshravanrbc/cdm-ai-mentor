11th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
Born in a rich family, extremely fortunate, and earns much wealth.
An exalted 11th Lord ensures birth in a very powerful family.
2nd House
The native will be endowed with all kinds of wealth and accomplishments, be charitable, religious, and always happy.
Earnings can come from banks, interest income, and commercial companies.
3rd House
The native will be skillful in all jobs, wealthy, and blessed with fraternal bliss.
May incur gout pains and will be the eldest or youngest in the family.
Rise in life through self-effort and hard work; earns through 3rd House professions.
4th House
Gains from maternal relatives, pilgrimages, and happiness from house and lands.
Example: Bill Gates.
5th House
Children will be happy, educated, and virtuous.
The native will be religious, happy, and prosperous through speculation.
Affliction may cause gambling tendencies.
6th House
The native will generally remain sickly, be cruel, live in foreign places, and be troubled by enemies.
Gains wealth through nursing, litigation, and the medical profession.
Wealth comes from hard work and overcoming enemies.
7th House
Gains through the wife’s relatives, liberal, virtuous, and sensual.
May reside in foreign lands after marriage.
Affliction can cause a troubled married life and extramarital relationships.
8th House
Suffers losses in undertakings but will be long-lived.
Wife may pass away before the native.
Indicates unexpected gains and inheritance.
Affliction may cause losses through thieves and cheats.
9th House
The native will be fortunate, skillful, truthful, honored by authorities, and wealthy.
Gains from father’s inheritance, luxury, and religious deeds.
10th House
Brings honor, high ranks, virtues, devotion to religion, truthfulness, and control over senses.
Leads to a big name, status, and major achievements.
11th House
Ensures gains in all undertakings, continuous learning, and increasing happiness.
12th House
Spends on good deeds, is sensual, and may have multiple wives.
Gains from foreign trade and export-import business.
Elder siblings may settle abroad or face health issues if afflicted.
Penalties from tax authorities are possible.
