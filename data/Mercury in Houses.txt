Mercury
Mercury is the prince in the solar system.
It is an earth element and controls two rashis: Gemini and Virgo.
Mercury stays about 4 weeks in a rashi.
It represents speech, intelligence, maternal uncles, short journeys, medical profession, trade, computers and the web, astrology, and knowledge of the shastras, accounts, mathematics, journalism, printing, and publishing.
Its metal is bronze, gem is emerald, color is green, and direction is north.
It is the karaka of voice and the main karaka of business and intellect.
Mercury in different houses
1st house
Mercury in the lagna (Ascendant) makes the person very intelligent and gives them a great sense of humor.
If Mercury is in its own rashi or exalted, it forms a great Rajyoga and makes the native very intelligent and a great mathematician.
Being a benefic planet in the lagna, it gives prosperity and fame to the native.
If Mercury is afflicted, the person has multiple thoughts and changes their mind frequently.
Afflicted Mercury in the 1st house makes the person characterless, and if it is with Rahu, it can cause depression.
2nd house
Mercury in the 2nd house makes the person intelligent and gives good education.
The person is born into a rich family and receives good initial education.
Mercury in this house makes the person very diplomatic in speech.
If afflicted, the native becomes a liar:
If with Ketu, the person becomes hesitant to speak.
If with Rahu, they speak too much.
If with Jupiter, they are very polite.
If with Saturn, they become a liar.
Mercury in the 2nd house makes the native business-minded and lucky.
If afflicted, the native has a tendency to steal money and commit financial malpractices.
3rd house
The 3rd house is the house of communication, and Mercury is its karaka.
Mercury in this house makes the native a very good communicator and skilled in fields like astrology, teaching, consultancy, mobile communication, and the media industry.
Mercury in the 3rd house is good for publication, spiritual activities, media, advertising, and communication.
Writers, poets, journalists, and orators generally have Mercury in or associated with the 3rd house.
Afflicted Mercury in this house makes the person a cheater with harsh speech, and they face trouble while traveling.
4th house
Mercury in the 4th house gives a career related to software, communication, trading, etc.
It makes the person very intelligent and interested in creative activities.
The person lives in a big house and has a cordial relationship with their mother.
Mercury in the 4th house gives properties, money, and happiness.
It can lead the native to foreign lands.
Afflicted Mercury here makes the person a cheater, characterless, and interested in pleasure-seeking.
The native doesn’t focus on one job and tries to multitask, making them restless.
5th house
The native is well-educated, very intelligent, a writer, and a good poet.
Mercury in fiery signs makes the person skilled in math, astrology, Vedic studies, and dancing.
In earthy signs, it is good for learning physics, palmistry, and grammar.
In water signs, it makes the native a clerk, language expert, and administrator.
Mercury in the 5th house is very good for children, but if afflicted in a female chart, it can cause abortions.
A badly placed Mercury in the 5th house makes the native confused and reduces decision-making power.
6th house
If well-placed, Mercury gives very good intellect and fame but makes the native combative.
It grants spiritual and divine knowledge from saints.
Afflicted Mercury gives poor intellect, mental issues, skin problems, poor digestion, and diseases in the genitals.
A badly placed Mercury in the 6th house creates obstacles in education.
7th house
Mercury, although a benefic planet, causes problems in marriage due to its dual nature and impotent qualities.
It gives issues with potency, and any relationship of Mercury with the 7th house can cause marital problems.
Since it’s a benefic planet, it makes the person intelligent, well-learned, obedient, and famous.
The spouse of the native may come from an influential family and be very intelligent and beautiful.
If Mercury is badly placed, it causes worries after marriage, conflicts, divorce, and even multiple marriages.
8th house
Mercury in the 8th house is the best placement for becoming an astrologer.
The native is spiritual and has a commanding speech.
Longevity increases, and the native lives a healthy life and dies peacefully.
Mercury in this house grants parental properties, inheritance, and wealth.
If afflicted, it makes the person a crook, liar, and filled with sexual desires.
Afflicted Mercury reduces the native's lifespan, causing mental disorders, skin problems in the lower body, and pains in the thighs and legs.
9th house
Mercury in the 9th house makes the person highly intelligent, a scientist, researcher, singer, music composer, and very rich.
It makes the person spiritual, philosophical, religious, and an astrologer.
It fosters good relationships with fathers and gurus.
If Mercury is badly placed, the native becomes rebellious toward religion, has a bad relationship with their father, and is fickle-minded.
The native becomes restless and tries to multitask but doesn't complete anything.
10th house
If exalted or in its own sign, Mercury forms a great Rajyoga here.
The native becomes famous in their profession, excels in communication, and is very close to influential people.
The native earns through communication, software, creative fields, or any intellectual profession.
The native has a sharp memory, is intelligent, and gains early career success.
If Mercury is badly placed, it causes career uncertainty, poor decisions, and recklessness in work.
11th house
The native is blessed with the best qualities.
Good Mercury here grants many friends and scientific knowledge.
The person is intelligent, famous, prosperous, happy, and likely to run a business.
Strong Mercury here fulfills all the native's desires, attracting love from the opposite sex.
12th house
This is a bad placement for Mercury.
The native has poor memory and weak decision-making power.
They may cheat people, disobey the law, and become lazy and uneducated.
The person travels a lot, especially to foreign countries.
If aspected by benefic planets, Mercury here gives a spiritual inclination and a strong sixth sense.
