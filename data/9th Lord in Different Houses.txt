9th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
A very good combination, as the 9th Lord in the Ascendant (both Kendra and Trikona) makes the native well-learned, famous, fortunate, and charming.
In females, it signifies a great wife.
If weak, it causes poverty, misfortune, lack of support from the wife, and marriage problems.
2nd House
Brings wealth through family, foreign connections, and family support.
The native is a learned scholar, dear to all, and enjoys happiness from the spouse.
If weak, there may be marital issues, family litigation, and humiliation.
3rd House
Indicates happiness from brothers, spiritual discourses, and interest in writing, publication, and oratory.
Travels to holy places and foreign lands.
If malefic, it causes problems in writing, loss of father’s wealth, and misfortune.
4th House
Combines Kendra and Trikona, ensuring all kinds of happiness, luxuries, houses, and wealth.
The native's mother is prosperous and long-lived.
If weak, it brings problems related to the mother, land, and happiness.
5th House
Strong for spirituality, learning, and talented children.
Indicates foreign education, mantra siddhi, and proximity to a guru.
If weak, causes problems for the father, children, and fortune.
6th House
The native may work in the judiciary or become a judge.
Father’s career improves after the native’s birth, but litigation and debts may arise.
If malefic, the native faces maternal uncle's troubles and enemies.
7th House
Brings happiness and fortune after marriage, along with foreign wealth.
The native is popular with the opposite sex and enjoys luxuries.
If weak, there will be marriage problems, poor health of the father, and a short first marriage.
8th House
Benefic placement brings spiritual insight, occult knowledge, and sudden gains.
Inclination toward research and soul-searching.
If weak, it causes misfortune, inheritance disputes, and health problems for the father.
9th House
In its own house, it grants all kinds of fortunes, wealth, and happiness.
Excellent for spirituality, leadership, and political roles.
If afflicted, it brings life challenges, stomach issues, and a lack of paternal support.
10th House
Considered a highly favorable placement, granting “Dharma Karam Adhipati Yoga.”
Brings power, high rank, fame, wealth, and charitable nature.
If weak, it causes job loss, humiliation, and punishment by the government.
11th House
Indicates financial gains, devotion to teachers, and charitable deeds.
Brings income through publications, entertainment, and government services.
If with malefics like Rahu or Saturn, wealth comes through illegal means.
12th House
Indicates foreign settlement and spiritual growth, living in a guru’s ashram.
If weak, it causes financial losses, father’s death, and excessive spending on auspicious acts.
