8th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
The native will be deprived of bodily pleasures, disrespectful to gods and Brahmins, and prone to wounds.
This placement can cause poverty, debt, poor health, and government displeasure.
Continuous problems in life and health issues are likely unless the 8th Lord is in its own rashi or exalted.
2nd House
The native will lack physical vigor, possess little wealth, and struggle to regain lost wealth.
Problems in the family and separation from the spouse are likely.
There may be issues with the eyes and teeth.
However, inheritance, hidden wealth, unexpected gains, and long life are possible due to the aspect on the 8th House.
3rd House
Fraternal happiness will be lacking, and the native will be lazy, devoid of energy, and without servants.
Problems with the younger brother are likely.
This combination creates various life challenges.
4th House
The native may be deprived of the mother, home, land, and happiness.
Betrayal from friends and a lack of peace of mind are indicated.
Benefic aspects can promote spiritual growth but cause struggles in acquiring property and vehicles.
5th House
The native will be dull-witted and have few children.
Good for studying history, ancient languages, and spiritual pursuits like mantra sadhana and samadhi.
If in its own house or exalted, negative results will be minimized.
6th House
The native will overcome opponents but have a diseased body.
Childhood may involve danger from snakes, drugs, or water.
7th House
This placement indicates divorce or separation and possibly the death of the first spouse.
Business losses and an irresponsible nature are likely.
8th House
The native will be long-lived unless the 8th Lord is weak, which gives a medium lifespan.
Blame and accusations may occur.
Benefic aspects promote spirituality.
9th House
This is dangerous for the father and can cause loss of religious faith.
The native may adopt a new religion, steal others' money, and be deceitful.
10th House
Lack of paternal bliss, instability in career, and frequent job changes are possible.
Careers in astrology or occult sciences may develop.
Many career obstacles are likely.
11th House
The native will lose money, especially if afflicted by malefics, which can cause poverty.
Benefic aspects may bring unexpected gains through speculation, gambling, or shares.
12th House
Money will be spent on negative pursuits, and the native may be short-lived.
Foreign residence, export-import business, and gains through trade are possible.
Benefic aspects promote spiritual growth.
