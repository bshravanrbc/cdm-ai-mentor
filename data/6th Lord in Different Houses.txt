6th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
If the 6th Lord is situated in the Ascendant, the native will be sickly, famous, inimical to relatives, adventurous, and virtuous.
It makes the native a high administrator, minister of war, or official concerned with prisons.
If weak, the person may become a thief, robber, or criminal.
Association with malefics can cause mental problems.
2nd House
The native will be adventurous, illustrious in the family, live in foreign lands, be happy, a good speaker, and always interested in work.
If weak, family life suffers, and the person may be unappreciated despite doing much for the family.
May have defective eyes, teeth, and speech issues, leading to poverty and debts.
3rd House
The native will be quarrelsome, uncompromising, bereft of courage, and inimical to brothers.
Servants will be disobedient.
4th House
The native will be devoid of maternal happiness, possibly due to an immoral mother.
They may be intelligent, a talebearer, jealous, and fickle-minded.
Education and maternal happiness suffer.
Property disputes and legal troubles are possible, indicating a miserable life.
5th House
The native’s wealth will fluctuate, and they may have enmity with their son and friends.
They will be happy, selfish, and kind.
If weak, both education and the son will suffer.
6th House
The native will have enmity with kinsmen but good friendships with others.
They will experience only moderate happiness in wealth matters.
The person develops great strength to handle uncontrollable challenges, and the maternal uncle may be wealthy.
7th House
The native will lack happiness in marriage, be famous, virtuous, honorable, adventurous, and wealthy.
The wife’s health may be poor, and impotency is possible.
This placement gives bad results in marriage.
8th House
The native will be sickly, inimical to the learned, desirous of others' wealth, and interested in others' spouses.
It gives a medium lifespan and gambling habits, which may lead to syphilis and troublesome periods.
It forms a Vipreet Raj Yoga, giving good results after initial hardships.
9th House
The father can become an enemy if the 6th Lord is afflicted.
The 6th Lord destroys the house where it sits.
If placed in the 9th House, it harms fortune and luck.
10th House
If strong, the native will be illustrious in the family, not very devoted to parents, a gifted speaker, and happy in foreign countries.
If afflicted, the native will engage in bad deeds, pretend to be religious, and be sinful.
Frequent job changes, transfers, or a shift from job to business is possible.
11th House
The native will gain from enemies, be virtuous, adventurous, and honorable.
If weak, poverty, gambling addiction, and a miserable life are likely.
12th House
Money will be spent on evil means, and the native will be inimical to the learned and violent toward animals.
A miserable life is predicted.
