12th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
If the 12th Lord is situated in the Ascendant, the native will be a spendthrift, weak in constitution, prone to phlegmatic disorders, and devoid of wealth and learning.
If benefically placed, this is a good combination for spiritual upliftment.
2nd House
The native will spend money on auspicious deeds, be religious, speak sweetly, and be blessed with virtues and happiness.
Family life will be disturbed, with weak eyesight and disrupted eating habits.
If afflicted, expenses may equal or exceed income, leaving no savings.
3rd House
The native will lack fraternal bliss, harbor hatred toward others, and be self-centered.
Problems for siblings, cowardice, and potential hearing issues are indicated.
4th House
Maternal happiness will be missing, with losses in land, conveyances, and houses over time.
Mental worries and strained relations with relatives will occur.
Problems related to property and conveyance are likely.
5th House
The native will lack sons and learning, but may spend money on pilgrimages in hopes of having children.
6th House
The native will face enmity with close relatives, be angry, sinful, and miserable.
A strong placement forms Vipreet Raj Yoga, making the native rich, healthy, and able to overcome enemies.
If afflicted, the person becomes unhappy, immoral, and hostile toward the mother.
7th House
The native will spend money because of the spouse and lack conjugal bliss, learning, and strength.
No education or property accumulation is expected.
8th House
This placement indicates gains, affable speech, good qualities, and a medium lifespan.
Forms Vipreet Raj Yoga, granting sudden gains and spiritual elevation.
9th House
The native will dishonor teachers, be inimical even to friends, and be self-serving.
Likely to live abroad, causing harm to the father and dissatisfaction with spouse, friends, and mentors.
10th House
The native will rise through influential people but experience moderate paternal bliss.
Likely professions include jailer, doctor, and working abroad or in multinational corporations.
11th House
Despite having gain combinations, the native will incur losses and experience only small profits from others' wealth.
12th House
The native will spend heavily, lack physical felicity, and be irritable and spiteful.
Astrological Insights
The effects of the Lords of the Houses depend on their strengths and weaknesses.
If a planet owns two signs, effects from both lordships must be considered.
A strong planet yields full results, while weaker planets provide partial effects.
