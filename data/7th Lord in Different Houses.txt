7th Lord in Different Houses
1st House
The native will be attached to his wife and may marry someone known since childhood.
Constant traveling and working with many women are indicated.
If badly placed, there can be more than one marriage.
In the dasha of the 7th Lord, there may be the death of the wife.
2nd House
The native may have many wives if the 7th Lord is in a dual sign or afflicted.
Gains wealth through the spouse but tends to procrastinate.
The Maraka dasha can be harmful to health.
3rd House
The native's brother may live abroad.
Adultery with the brother’s wife and misfortune to the co-born are possible.
4th House
A happy wife brings all kinds of domestic happiness.
High academic qualifications and many vehicles are indicated.
If weak or afflicted, there will be no domestic happiness and problems with conveyances.
5th House
The native will be honorable, virtuous, delighted, and wealthy.
Early marriage, love marriage, and a wealthy spouse are possible.
If afflicted, there will be no children.
6th House
The native’s wife will be sickly or face health issues.
The native may be angry, inimical toward the spouse, and devoid of happiness.
This placement can indicate divorce, especially if afflicted, and two marriages are possible.
7th House
The native will be happy, courageous, skillful, and intelligent but may suffer from windy diseases.
A charming personality and a wife from a good family are indicated.
If weak, there will be marriage problems, loneliness, or even separation.
8th House
Marital happiness will be lacking.
The spouse may be afflicted by diseases, bad-natured, and disobedient.
9th House
The native will have multiple relationships but remain attached to the spouse.
The father may live abroad, and the native will undertake many deeds.
In a female chart, an afflicted 7th Lord can cause marriage disturbances, divorce, separation, or canceled engagements.
10th House
The native will be religious, wealthy, and blessed with sons.
Professional success abroad and working with or receiving help from the spouse are indicated.
This is a good placement as it involves two Kendras.
11th House
The native will gain wealth through the spouse, who will be very rich.
Multiple marriages, legal or otherwise, are possible.
12th House
The native will be miserly, and the spouse will be a spendthrift.
Earnings may come through trading textiles or garments.
Illicit affairs, separation, late marriage, or the death of the spouse are possible.
