Saturn in Houses
Saturn is a sterile, slow, icy, binding, invariable, arid, protective, rigid, fearful, and mysterious planet.
It governs the zodiac signs Capricorn (Makar Rashi) and Aquarius (Kumbh Rashi).
Its colors are Black, Dark Blue, and Dark Brown.
Saturn is an air element and controls all the muscles in the body.
It stays 2.5 years in one rashi, and its transit through three rashis is called Sade Sati.
Saturn is considered the Chief Governor for Longevity, but being a malefic planet, it can cause delays, difficulties, and disharmony.
Element: Iron, Gem: Blue Sapphire, Direction: West.
It is responsible for technical education, mining, machinery, metals, oil, aviation, and judiciary.
Saturn in Different Houses
1st house
Makes the person slow-moving, thoughtful, and strong-minded.
Causes problems with hair and a slim physique.
Delayed marriage is likely.
If in its own sign or exalted, gives immense power to conquer enemies.
If in an enemy sign, can cause separation from the spouse.
Increases the chance of settling in a foreign land.
2nd house
Indicates good health and large wealth accumulation.
Wealth may come from illegal sources like gambling, lottery, or shares.
May face diseases related to the face and problems with the eyes.
Harsh speech and tendency toward tamasic food, liquor, and tobacco.
The native may stay away from family and birthplace.
3rd house
Possible loss of brothers or no younger siblings.
If exalted, ensures good relations with siblings and strong communication skills.
May face obstacles but will be ambitious and a good orator.
Indicates a long life and interest in astrology or spirituality.
4th house
May cause loss of the mother or strained relationships.
If in its own house or exalted, brings property and prestigious vehicles.
The native may face mental distress, lack of happiness, and childhood illnesses.
Can result in living away from the native place.
5th house
May cause childlessness, delayed children, or problems with children.
If strong, can indicate many romantic relationships.
Creates a lack of learning, wealth, and happiness.
The native may wander aimlessly and be of an evil nature.
6th house
Destroys enemies and grants fame.
Indicates losses related to uncles.
Can cause problems with the lower body and marriage issues.
Enhances determination and resilience in overcoming obstacles.
7th house
Indicates a lean-bodied spouse and delayed marriage.
Can cause problems in marriage and multiple diseases.
The native may experience political success or foreign honors.
Intimacy with multiple partners is possible if exalted or in its own sign.
8th house
Indicates long life but defective eyesight.
May cause poverty, piles, food scarcity, and cruel behavior.
The native may experience frequent losses and separation from friends.
9th house
Indicates a sinful nature if afflicted.
The father’s lifespan depends on Saturn’s strength.
The native may be irreligious, cruel, childless, and troublesome to others.
Can indicate a legal mind, charitable work, and ceremonial thinking.
10th house
Indicates professional success, great work ethic, and foreign travel.
May face career obstacles if associated with malefics.
If supported by benefics, the native will be wealthy, famous, and respected.
May indulge in sinful deeds but overcome enemies successfully.
11th house
Ensures great wealth, influence, and political success.
Indicates ownership of large properties and fearlessness.
The native will be wealthy and respected despite facing some obstacles.
12th house
Indicates interest in yoga, occult sciences, and foreign travels.
If associated with benefics, brings happiness, good eyesight, and heavenly rewards after death.
If afflicted, causes poverty, wasteful expenses, and body deformities.
Indicates a restless mind, spiritual inclinations, and potential travel abroad.
