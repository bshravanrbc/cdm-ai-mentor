2nd Lord in Different Houses
1st House
When the 2nd Lord is strong and with benefics, the native makes wealth through personal efforts.
If weak, there will be more expenses.
The native will be endowed with sons and wealth but may be inimical to his family, lustful, hard-hearted, and engaged in others' jobs.
2nd House
The native will be wealthy, proud, and blessed with a very good family.
Malefic aspects may disturb married life and cause problems with the wife and family.
A well-placed malefic here may lead to academic distinction in scientific fields due to deep research associations.
3rd House
When the Lord of money goes to the house of creative arts and talents, the native can earn through art, drama, music, and with the help of brothers.
A malefic aspect or placement may cause wealth loss due to brothers.
4th House
Wealth is generated through property, mother, agriculture, conveyance, lands, education, and vehicles.
The native gets inheritance, is truthful, and kind-hearted.
If conjunct with Jupiter or in its exaltation sign, the native may achieve king-like status.
5th House
A well-placed 2nd Lord gives wealth through lottery, shares, temples, education, children, and the government.
Malefic associations may cause loss of wealth through these sources.
6th House
This placement is favorable for earning through the medical profession.
If badly placed, the native may incur continuous debts and struggle to repay loans.
If conjunct with benefics, the native may gain wealth through enemies; malefics may cause losses.
7th House
Wealth may come from foreign sources, business in foreign countries, partnerships, the wife, and women.
The native may traverse others' wives and practice medicine if well-placed.
8th House
The 2nd Lord aspects the 2nd House from here, making it favorable despite the loss-oriented 8th House.
Wealth through inheritance is likely, but marital discord and lack of happiness from elder siblings may occur.

11th House
The native may earn through elder brothers.
The 2nd Lord in the 11th House ensures all kinds of wealth, gains, honor, and fame.
12th House

Wealth is easily spent, leaving little savings.
The native may be adventurous, dependent on others, and lack happiness from the eldest son.
