Introduction to Rashis (Signs)
Aries (Mesha)
● Symbol: Sheep
● Lord: Mars | Element: Fire | Char Rashi
● Physical Traits: Dark complexion, brown eyes, strong body, dwarf or medium height
● Personality: Ambitious, courageous, self-respecting, short-tempered, impatient, determined
with direction
Taurus (Vrishabha)
● Symbol: Ox
● Lord: Venus | Element: Earth | Sthir Rashi
● Physical Traits: Charming, good-looking
● Personality: Cool, tolerant, slow but steady worker, easily makes friends, fond of luxuries,
laborious
Gemini (Mithuna)
● Symbol: Pair of male and female
● Lord: Mercury | Element: Air | Dual Rashi
● Physical Traits: Thin, tall, medium complexion, dark eyes
● Personality: Intelligent, good communicator, humorous, multi-skilled, fast learner, talkative,
dual-minded
Cancer (Karka)
● Symbol: Crab
● Lord: Moon | Element: Water | Char Rashi
● Personality: Sympathetic, emotional, intuitive, moody, caring, seeks comfort for loved ones,
avoids conflicts
Leo (Simha)
● Symbol: Lion
● Lord: Sun | Element: Fire | Sthir Rashi
● Physical Traits: Tall, strong physique, broad chest
● Personality: Courageous, ambitious, independent, autocratic, suspicious, strong-willed,
occasionally ill-tempered
Virgo (Kanya)
● Lord: Mercury | Element: Earth | Dual Rashi
● Physical Traits: Practical, intelligent, shy, sensitive, logical
● Personality: Observant, patient, studious, soft-spoken, discrete, planning mind, perfectionist
Libra (Tula)
● Symbol: Balancing Scales
● Lord: Venus | Element: Air | Char Rashi
● Personality: Balanced, intelligent, sensitive, learned, helpful, action-oriented, social worker
Scorpio (Vrischika)
● Symbol: Scorpion
● Lord: Mars | Element: Water | Sthir Rashi
● Personality: Short-tempered, self-confident, serious, jealous, courageous, fearless, prone to
addictions
Sagittarius (Dhanu)
● Symbol: Archer (Man on Horse)
● Lord: Jupiter | Element: Fire | Dual Rashi
● Personality: Happy, enterprising, logical, restless, curious, adventurous, social, teacher-like
Capricorn (Makar)
● Symbol: Goat
● Lord: Saturn | Element: Earth | Char Rashi
● Personality: Hardworking, sincere, disciplined, ambitious, strong-willed, reserved, determined,
persistent
Aquarius (Kumbha)
● Symbol: Pot Pouring Water
● Lord: Saturn | Element: Air | Sthir Rashi
● Personality: Imaginative, serious, spiritual, unprejudiced, tolerant, practical, intuitive,
quick-witted
Pisces (Meena)
● Symbol: Two Fish Chasing Each Other
● Lord: Jupiter | Element: Water | Dual Rashi
● Personality: Trusting, simple, emotional, imaginative, practical, intuitive, caring, courageous at
times
