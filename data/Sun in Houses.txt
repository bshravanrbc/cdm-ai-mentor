Surya - Sun
Sun or Surya is a royal planet and the king in astrology.
He represents the soul, will power, father, paternal relations, king, or high officials.
Surya is a fire element; Leo is its rashi & it stays 30 days in a rashi.
It has only 7Â° aspect.
Sun represents all government work, jobs, politicians, bureaucrats, etc.
Sun is the karaka of all bones in the body.
Surya signifies leadership, authority, dominance, strength, and fatherly qualities.
Color of Sun: Bright Red
Sun in Different Houses
1st House
These people with Sun in the 1st house or Ascendant are born leaders, but they should avoid overshadowing others.
Gives wisdom and a positive attitude toward life.
Being the king and a fire element, the Sun can make the native short-tempered or angry.
Good for a political career, leadership roles, etc.
These people have high ambitions in life and work hard to achieve them.
2nd House
Natives with Sun in the 2nd house are effective talkers and leave a lasting impression.
They are intellectual and inclined toward research and scientific pursuits.
Good orators, but this position is generally not good for marriage and family matters.
Can display a rebellious attitude toward family.
If afflicted or aspected by malefics, the native may become a spendthrift and a rebel.
3rd House
Bravery, vigor, and strength are bestowed on the native.
Good speaker but will speak only when necessary.
Conflict with younger siblings is possible.
Energetic, optimistic, religious, and hardworking.
Honored by government, poetic, and interested in occultism, meditation, and sports.
4th House
The native values cultural and family traditions.
Leads a stable, peaceful life even in difficult times due to inner calmness.
Government jobs, politics, or bureaucratic careers are favored.
Afflicted Sun: May bring greed, immoral tendencies, or conflicts with the mother.
Native may live away from the native place.
5th House
Natives are mentally strong, adventurous, and courageous.
May be inclined toward acting, writing, or performing arts.
Afflicted Sun: Can cause educational setbacks or problems with children.
Natives are devoted to children but may face strained relationships with them.
Wealth accumulation is possible through personal efforts.
6th House
Natives are determined, sincere, and hardworking.
Exceptional problem-solving skills and ability to overcome enemies.
Attached to work accomplishments and show pride in their efforts.
Inclination toward yoga, good relations with family, and pursuit of good deeds.
7th House
Challenging married life with a desire for luxuries from the spouse.
May become selfish, furious, and sensitive to rejection.
Possibility of second marriage, venereal diseases, or piles/fistula.
Helpful by nature, but may force advice on others.
Popular in the workplace.
8th House
Foreign residence is likely.
Talkative and impatient, sometimes speaking without purpose.
Luck doesn't favor consistently.
Short-tempered, cunning, and mentally restless.
Health issues: Venereal diseases, widowhood, and fire-related accidents.
9th House
Truthful, religious, and fond of spiritual teachers and deities.
Weak health in childhood but wealthy in youth.
Troubled relationship with father and possible defamation from the government.
Idealistic, visionary, and wise, but may face conflicts with authorities.
10th House
Talented, happy, generous, and egoistic.
Close to politicians and higher authorities.
Bestows power and authority, enabling the native to dominate.
Destined for recognition and leadership in government-related work or high-ranking jobs.
11th House
Wealthy, fond of the opposite sex, and charitable.
Strong influence and affluence in life.
Humanitarian approach, with a happy and satisfied life.
12th House
Foolish and whimsical, rude, and interested in others' spouses.
Introverted, always seeking personal space.
Inimical relationship with father, defective eyesight, and lack of wealth or children.
Loss of wealth through government or other authorities.
